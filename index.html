Common Gardening Measurements

  • General lawn fertilizer: 25-35g per square meter (0.8-1.1 oz per square yard)
  • Starter fertilizer: 50g per square meter (1.6 oz per square yard)
  • Slow-release fertilizer: 15-25g per square meter (0.5-0.8 oz per square yard)
  • Liquid lawn feed: 10ml per litre of water (1.3 fl oz per gallon)
  • Algaecide: 5-10ml per 1000 litres (0.7-1.3 fl oz per 250 gallons)
  • Water clarifier: 5ml per 1000 litres (0.7 fl oz per 250 gallons)
  • Beneficial bacteria: 10ml per 1000 litres (1.3 fl oz per 250 gallons)
  • Dechlorinator: 5ml per 1000 litres (0.7 fl oz per 250 gallons)
  • Liquid plant food: 5ml per litre of water (0.7 fl oz per gallon)
  • Tomato fertilizer: 7ml per litre of water (0.9 fl oz per gallon)
  • Granular fertilizer: 30-60g per square meter (1-2 oz per square yard)
  • Slow-release fertilizer: 1-2g per litre of potting soil (0.035-0.07 oz per quart)
  • Standard teaspoon: approximately 5ml or 5g
  • Standard tablespoon: approximately 15ml or 15g
  • Small fertilizer scoop: approximately 5-10g
  • Medium fertilizer scoop: approximately 20-30g
  • Large fertilizer scoop: approximately 40-50g
  • Coffee scoop: approximately 10g or 15ml
